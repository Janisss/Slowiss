To work as a nurse or midwife in the UK you are required to register with Nursing and Midwifery Council.

Professional qualification recognition

Ministry of Health of the Slovak Republic validates equality of medical professions to applicants that achieved their qualification in Slovak Republic or in former Czechoslovakia and want to work in other member state.

In case that your training programme started after 1. May 2004:

You do not need Confirmation of qualification recognition, because of the automatic recognition based on Directive 2005/36/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council on the recognition of professional qualifications.

In case that your training programme started between 1 January 1993 and 30 April 2004:

It is required to obtain a certificate from the Ministerstvo zdravotníctva which must confirm that you have effectively and lawfully been engaged in nursing practice for at least three consecutive years during the last five years. The certificate must state it was awarded to you in accordance with article 23(1) of Directive 2005/36/EC.

In case that your training programme started before 1st of January 1993:

It is required to obtain a certificate from Ministerstvo zdravotníctva which must confirm that you have effectively and lawfully been engaged in nursing practice for at least three consecutive years during the last five years. The certificate must state that it was awarded to you in accordance with article 23(3) of Directive 2005/36/EC.
