To work as a nurse in Switzerland you need to be from EU or EFTA country, to have a B2 level of German/French/Italian and to have achieved a certain qualification. You can find the list of considered qualifications here.

Aspects considered by Swiss Red Cross

- Nationality
- Date when qualification completed
- Country of origin
- When and where apprenticeship completed


We assist you with whole process of application. You just need to send us documents confirming your qualification and we will proceed it further to Swiss red cross that will assess wether it is sufficient to start application process in Switzerland.
